# Mob Defenders
Mob Defenders stop mobs from spawning within 50 blocks of the sign.

> This machine does not need any power to operate.

## Multiblock
### Materials Required

- 1x sign
- 1x diamond block
- 1x glass
- 6x glass pane
- 4x stone brick
- 5x stone brick stairs
