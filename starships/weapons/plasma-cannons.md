Plasma Cannons are similar to Laser Cannons, however they are much more powerfull but also more limited in direction they can fire.

|Property|Value|
|---|---|
|Power Usage|2500|
|Range|160|
|Type|Light Weapon|
|Impact|1 explosion, 4.0 power|
|Shield Impact|12|
|Firing Arc|15 Degrees|
|Ammo|None|
